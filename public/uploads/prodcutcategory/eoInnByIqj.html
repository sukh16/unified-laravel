Switches & Relays

When you choose Sensata switches and relays, you gain a double advantage — extremely high quality devices plus our collaborative design process.

View All Products

Enhance your designs with our robust solutions and deep expertise.

Sensata switches and relays are engineered to withstand the harshest conditions — from deep space to the ocean floor. Wherever you need great performance, we make it practical, with:

  • A portfolio of devices encompassing AC and DC output and numerous mounting styles
  • Our unique, engineer-to- engineer design process 

At Sensata, we encourage you to collaborate with our technical and sales experts. Discover how they can help you create customized solutions for your most demanding applications.

Cynergy3 Reed Relays

Sensata | Cynergy3 produces a wide range of reed relays in a variety of package sizes for high voltage or radio frequency applications. They play an important role in the field of medicine being used in defibrillators that save lives every day. Other applications include use in semi-conductor and insulation testing, military in-the-field communications, electrosurgical equipment and charging of electric vehicles.
